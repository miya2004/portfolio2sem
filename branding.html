Prototype

In the first week me and my group started doing stylescapes and at first I made it with Justin and Aleksa and later on we got a feedback to make an individual style cape. Based on my competitor analysis that I made I end up combining everything from the websites and the ideas I had and I started with my prototype but after the first review the client got more ideas and also made a surveyso the next prototype changed a little bit and it stayed like that till the end.

To guide our design process, I created two style scapes: one with my teammates and another individually. Before doing the style scape, I researched what style scape should look like. Style scapes are visual representations that help define the aesthetic direction of a project.

Color and Typography Choices: For my style scape, I chose colors that resonate with our project's theme. Blue was selected for its association with doctor uniforms, while grey reflects nurse uniforms. Black and white were used for text to ensure clarity. For typography, I opted for fonts that enhance readability and lend a professional appearance to the design rather than childish.
